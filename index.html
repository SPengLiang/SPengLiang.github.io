Liang Peng (彭亮)

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Deng Cai. During 2018-2020, my major was software engineering, and I changed my major to computer science and technology in 2020. I got my bachelor's degree from measurement and control technology and instruments at Hefei University of Technology in 2018.

I am interested in 3D scene understanding in self-driving, especially on 3D object detection. I am an intern at Fabu Inc. (from 2019 to now).
